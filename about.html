About this Project

By Yousuf and Sehrish | Feb 20, 2021

The Internet powers our modern life. In many places, access to the Internet is considered a human right. Revolutions have been won with its help, armies toppled, regimes overturned. Its power has ushered in a new era of human society, the Information Age. Yet, as pervasive as the Internet is, the details of its workings are a mystery to most. On this site, we uncover these secrets and delve into the technologies that power this modern marvel. Hopefully by demystifying it, we can more easily leverage its power.
