Cyber Security and Crime

Cybercrime causes many personal, financial, and national security problems. People who commit these crimes can be anyone who has the knowledge to do so. Viruses work similarly to biological viruses where they can harm one computer and spread to others. Viruses can infect computers through a fake installation of an application, or an attacker can install itself without user permission. Hackers can take over many computers and use a bot net to attack and take down websites. This is known as a Distributed Denial of Service where hackers inundate websites with too many requests. Billons and trillions of requests can overload computers and stop them from responding.

Common cyber crime

Another example of cybercrime is phishing. These scams are when hackers use spam email to trick people into sharing personal information. 90% of the time a system gets hacked is not because of a security bug but because of human mistakes.

How do I protect myself?

  • Use strong passwords
  • Check for authentic web addresses
  • Install system security updates often
  • Don’t install software you don’t trust
